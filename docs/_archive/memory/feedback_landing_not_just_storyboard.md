The 4-Act storyboard is loved but the landing page cannot BE a storyboard alone. It must first be a SaaS landing page that sells.

**Why:** The user looked at the current home page spec (00-home.md) and felt it was too much storyboard, not enough SaaS landing. References: Vercel, Supabase, Notion, monday, Superhuman, Framer — all have storyboard-like elements but within a conventional SaaS landing structure.

**How to apply:** When designing/implementing the home page, ensure it has all standard SaaS landing sections (hero, product overview, social proof, pricing, FAQ, CTA) with the storyboard as an enhancement within that structure, not as a replacement for it. The Atos are the narrative vehicle but the page must still read as a landing page to someone who scrolls quickly.
