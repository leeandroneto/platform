# 15. Archetypes curados (Passo 1)

> Status: **18 archetypes selecionados** (13 originais + 5 borderline confirmados, seção 14-18 abaixo)
> Princípio: maximum diversity (não "as melhores", mas "as que cobrem todo o espectro")
> Data: 2026-05-20
> Complemento mobile-first: `docs/design-system/19-mobile-first-additions.md` (+5 = 23 total)

## TL;DR

**18 archetypes desktop-first** selecionados: 13 originais + 5 borderline adicionados (user decision 2026-05-20, seção 14-18 abaixo). Mais 5 mobile-first em `19-mobile-first-additions.md` = **23 total**. 58 marcas descartadas porque o vibe foi coberto por uma selecionada equivalente. Espectro coberto: dev-tools dark technical, warm-cream editorial, photography-first cinematic luxury, bold athletic commerce, magazine brutalist, AI sunset gradient, fintech serious-electric, friendly mascot SaaS, opacity-driven warm minimalism, all-mono terminal-native, financial-confident enterprise, hyper-monochrome austere luxury automotive, e telecom heavy-display photography.

## Lista final

### 1. linear.app

- **Vibe primário:** dev-tools dark, dense, software-craft documentation
- **Dimensões caracterizadoras:**
  - Mood: technical, quietly luxurious
  - Density: compact (cards densos, hairlines, dense panels)
  - Shape: soft (cards arredondados leves)
  - Typography: sans-only (Linear Display custom, 500-700, tracking negativo)
  - Photography: framed (product UI screenshots em panels escuros, sem photography atmosferica)
  - Motion: eased (focus rings, micro)
  - Color temp: cool-dark (#010102 — o mais escuro da coleção)
  - Shadow: zero (hairlines fazem o trabalho)
  - Border: hairline-heavy (#23252a)
- **Vertical fit hipotético:** coaching técnico, business pro, dev-tools (futuro), ingles.app premium
- **Por que selecionada:** representa o ápice do "dark technical product canvas" — single accent lavender, zero decoração, dense panel system. Referência para qualquer marca que queira voz "engineered/quiet/serious".
- **Marcas descartadas com vibe similar:** vercel, cursor (dark mode side), composio, voltagent, supabase (dark side), raycast, sanity (sanity quase entrou — ver nota)

### 2. notion

- **Vibe primário:** illustration-rich friendly workspace, pastel feature cards
- **Dimensões caracterizadoras:**
  - Mood: warm, gentle, playful-but-pro
  - Density: comfortable
  - Shape: rounded (cards 12-16px, navy hero band)
  - Typography: mixed (Notion-Sans Inter-based, sticky-note dots como brand)
  - Photography: composite (live workspace UI mockups + sticky-note dots + mesh wire illustrations)
  - Motion: eased
  - Color temp: warm-light (navy hero + pastel features)
  - Shadow: single-tier whisper
  - Border: invisible (cards via tint, não border)
- **Vertical fit hipotético:** coaching, idiomas (yoga.app friendly), business consumer
- **Por que selecionada:** representa "all-in-one SaaS friendly", pastel feature cards, illustration-rich. Distinct de Notion porque trabalha hierarquia 4 tiers de pricing + live UI mockups.
- **Marcas descartadas com vibe similar:** miro (similar yellow-pastel sticky-note vibe), slack (aubergine version), intercom (orange editorial-soft variant), cal.com (mais simples), figma (mais brutalist com color blocks)

### 3. stripe

- **Vibe primário:** financial infrastructure, atmospheric gradient mesh + tabular precision
- **Dimensões caracterizadoras:**
  - Mood: formal, technical-elegant
  - Density: comfortable→compact (tabular numerics)
  - Shape: sharp (tight-radius pills, tight-radius cards)
  - Typography: sans-only (Sohne 300 thin + tabular figures)
  - Photography: composite (atmospheric gradient mesh upper-third de toda página)
  - Motion: eased, subtle
  - Color temp: cool-light primary (deep navy + electric indigo)
  - Shadow: zero on chrome
  - Border: hairline
- **Vertical fit hipotético:** business pro, financial verticals, "fintech-grade" feeling
- **Por que selecionada:** referência canônica de "financial-infrastructure elegant". A combinação atmospheric mesh + tabular figures + Sohne thin é única — nenhuma outra coberta replica.
- **Marcas descartadas com vibe similar:** coinbase (single-blue mais sobrio), kraken (purple version), wise (lime), revolut (fintech-brochure mais consumer), binance (yellow CTA fintech), mongodb (green-pill dual mode)

### 4. nike

- **Vibe primário:** bold athletic commerce, towering uppercase Futura
- **Dimensões caracterizadoras:**
  - Mood: energetic, kinetic, absolute
  - Density: comfortable (8px grid retail)
  - Shape: sharp + pill mix (pill CTAs em retail chrome)
  - Typography: serif-display? — NO, Futura uppercase extremo (display contrast tipográfico maximo)
  - Photography: full-bleed (editorial campaign imagery dominates)
  - Motion: cinematic (kinetic athletic)
  - Color temp: high-contrast (pure black + white, single gray)
  - Shadow: zero
  - Border: invisible (chrome neutral, fotografia carrega)
- **Vertical fit hipotético:** **fitness (desafit.app dia 1!)**, athletic wellness, performance coaching
- **Por que selecionada:** ÚNICA com voz athletic/kinetic + photography-burned uppercase display. Referência crítica para desafit.app (vertical fitness). Não há substituto.
- **Marcas descartadas com vibe similar:** adidas-equivalent ausente; bmw-m e ferrari são "motorsport" mas não athletic; lululemon-equivalent ausente.

### 5. apple

- **Vibe primário:** museum-gallery photography-first, edge-to-edge product tiles
- **Dimensões caracterizadoras:**
  - Mood: editorial, luxury-restrained
  - Density: spacious (gallery breathing)
  - Shape: soft (cards arredondados, geometry quiet)
  - Typography: sans-only (SF Pro Display, negative letter-spacing)
  - Photography: full-bleed (alternating light/dark tiles)
  - Motion: eased (single signature shadow under product)
  - Color temp: neutral (light/dark alternates, single Action Blue #0066cc)
  - Shadow: single-tier (the one drop-shadow under product)
  - Border: invisible (UI chrome recedes)
- **Vertical fit hipotético:** premium any-vertical, luxury coaching, premium retail-style
- **Por que selecionada:** "alternating light/dark tiles + UI chrome recedes" é seu próprio archetype. Tesla é parente (radical subtraction) mas Apple tem o museum-gallery alternance que Tesla não tem (Tesla é all-white minimal showroom).
- **Marcas descartadas com vibe similar:** tesla (single-vehicle showroom variant), playstation (3-surface trailer variant), bmw (cream-tinted automotive corporate)

### 6. wired

- **Vibe primário:** editorial magazine ported to web, serif display + sans metadata
- **Dimensões caracterizadoras:**
  - Mood: editorial, formal-print
  - Density: compact (long-form reading)
  - Shape: sharp (square containers, magazine grid)
  - Typography: serif-display (tall narrow custom display serif + humanist serif body + clean sans metadata)
  - Photography: framed (magazine editorial layout)
  - Motion: none (very little marketing chrome)
  - Color temp: high-contrast (stark black wordmark, white canvas)
  - Shadow: zero
  - Border: hairline (magazine rules)
- **Vertical fit hipotético:** idiomas (ingles.app premium editorial), coaching premium long-form
- **Por que selecionada:** ÚNICA com "magazine-print-on-web" serif-heavy voz. Theverge é brutalist-magazine mas dark/loud (oposto). Cohere e elevenlabs têm editorial calm mas SEM serif display tradicional.
- **Marcas descartadas com vibe similar:** theverge (dark brutalist variant — kept as separate archetype #11), cohere (closer to editorial but sans-display), claude (serif-display mas warm-coral mood — kept as #12)

### 7. spacex

- **Vibe primário:** mission-oriented aerospace austere, full-bleed photo/video
- **Dimensões caracterizadoras:**
  - Mood: brutalist-austere, mission-driven
  - Density: spacious (single ghost pill per band)
  - Shape: sharp (uppercase D-DIN tight vertical leading)
  - Typography: sans-only (D-DIN all-caps eyebrow microtext)
  - Photography: full-bleed (rockets, Mars landscapes — imagery IS the content)
  - Motion: cinematic (immersive)
  - Color temp: high-contrast (pure black + white)
  - Shadow: zero
  - Border: invisible (ghost outlined pill button per band)
- **Vertical fit hipotético:** premium high-stakes coaching (executive), mission-driven brands
- **Por que selecionada:** representa "imagery does ALL the work" pushed à extrema — single ghost pill, all-caps eyebrows, video heroes. Único exemplo de "anti-marketing aesthetic" pleno.
- **Marcas descartadas com vibe similar:** lamborghini (gold accent quebra a austere), bugatti (mais luxury European), ferrari (Rosso Corsa accent breaks austere)

### 8. mistral.ai

- **Vibe primário:** AI sunset-gradient warm cream + horizontal closing stripe
- **Dimensões caracterizadoras:**
  - Mood: warm, atmospheric, near-editorial
  - Density: comfortable
  - Shape: soft
  - Typography: serif-display tendency (elegant near-serif voice hero)
  - Photography: composite (mountain + atmospheric gradients)
  - Motion: eased (gradient sunset)
  - Color temp: warm (cream-yellow + saturated orange CTA)
  - Shadow: single-tier
  - Border: invisible (gradient handles separation)
- **Vertical fit hipotético:** wellness brands, yoga.app, lifestyle warmth
- **Por que selecionada:** ÚNICA com "atmospheric warm sunset" como signature chrome system. Cursor (warm cream) e replicate (warm + orange) são próximos mas sem o sunset gradient + horizontal stripe.
- **Marcas descartadas com vibe similar:** cursor (warm cream, mas dev-tools), claude (warm cream serif, mas coral/cream pairing diferente — selecionado #12), replicate (warm cream + orange ML lab — também próximo)

### 9. wise

- **Vibe primário:** Scandinavian fintech magazine, heavy display + lime
- **Dimensões caracterizadoras:**
  - Mood: warm-fintech, magazine
  - Density: comfortable (rounded white cards on pale green-tint canvas)
  - Shape: rounded (cards)
  - Typography: sans-only (unusually heavy near-black weight 900 at 64-126px)
  - Photography: framed
  - Motion: eased
  - Color temp: warm-neutral (pale green-tint + lime accent)
  - Shadow: single-tier
  - Border: invisible/hairline
- **Vertical fit hipotético:** consumer-financial brands, fitness-business hybrid
- **Por que selecionada:** combina display sans EXTREMAMENTE pesado (weight 900) com tom warm magazine — combo único. Nenhuma outra coberta usa weight 900 como signature.
- **Marcas descartadas com vibe similar:** zapier (warm cream + orange + Degular display — also unique, see note), vodafone (heavy 800 mas red telecom — kept #13)

### 10. figma

- **Vibe primário:** monochrome canvas + oversized hand-cut pastel color blocks
- **Dimensões caracterizadoras:**
  - Mood: playful + technical (tool for serious work, made by people who like color)
  - Density: comfortable
  - Shape: sharp + pill (pill CTAs)
  - Typography: sans-only (figmaSans variable)
  - Photography: composite (color-block panels as sticky-notes)
  - Motion: eased
  - Color temp: high-contrast monochrome interrupted by saturated lime/lavender/cream/mint/pink panels
  - Shadow: zero
  - Border: invisible/hairline
- **Vertical fit hipotético:** creative coaching, design education, idiomas playful
- **Por que selecionada:** ÚNICA arquitetura "monochrome frame + color block interruption" como signature. Combina serenidade técnica com joy cromático — voz não replicada por nenhuma outra.
- **Marcas descartadas com vibe similar:** miro (mais sticky-note pastel sem brutalismo monochrome), notion (mais 4-tier pricing-driven), clay (color blocks mas claymation 3D)

### 11. theverge

- **Vibe primário:** brutalist tech-magazine club-night, acid + ultraviolet on dark
- **Dimensões caracterizadoras:**
  - Mood: brutalist, energetic, loud
  - Density: compact (timeline StoryStream)
  - Shape: rounded (20-40px radius color-block tiles)
  - Typography: sans-only display brutal (Manuka 107px — loudest in mainstream tech media)
  - Photography: composite (color-block story tiles as rave flyers)
  - Motion: eased/none
  - Color temp: cool-dark (#131313 + acid-mint #3cffd0 + ultraviolet #5200ff)
  - Shadow: zero (hairline 1px borders do the work)
  - Border: accent-bordered (mint + ultraviolet borders)
- **Vertical fit hipotético:** youth-energy fitness, creative idiomas, edgy coaching
- **Por que selecionada:** ÚNICA "brutalist editorial" — Manuka 107px headlines + hazard-tape accents é unsubstitutable. Sentry tem mood subversive mas illustrated/mascots; theverge é puro brutalism.
- **Marcas descartadas com vibe similar:** sentry (illustrated subversive mascots variant), framer (vibrant gradient panels variant), nvidia (angular green-accent but more enterprise-disciplined)

### 12. claude

- **Vibe primário:** warm tinted cream + serif display + coral CTAs (humanist AI)
- **Dimensões caracterizadoras:**
  - Mood: warm, gentle, humanist
  - Density: comfortable
  - Shape: soft (rounded cards)
  - Typography: serif-display (Copernicus/Tiempos Headline slab-serif h1/h2 + humanist sans body)
  - Photography: framed (code editor mockups + model showcase cards)
  - Motion: eased
  - Color temp: warm (cream canvas + warm coral CTAs + dark navy surfaces)
  - Shadow: single-tier
  - Border: hairline
- **Vertical fit hipotético:** **yoga.app warmth, coaching humanist, idiomas gentle**, wellness premium
- **Por que selecionada:** ÚNICA combinando serif slab-display + cream/coral pairing (deliberadamente warm onde maioria AI é cool blue). Cobre o vibe "warm humanist tech" intransferível.
- **Marcas descartadas com vibe similar:** cursor (warm cream mas sem serif slab), elevenlabs (off-white editorial mas atmospheric photographic, não serif slab), zapier (warm cream coffee ink + Degular — also distinct, see #13 alternative)

### 13. vodafone

- **Vibe primário:** telecom super-brand heavy uppercase + signature red CTA on photography
- **Dimensões caracterizadoras:**
  - Mood: formal, confident-corporate
  - Density: comfortable (editorial photography hero bands + clean white content)
  - Shape: rounded (some pills, mostly square cards)
  - Typography: sans-only (Vodafone display sans 800 — impossibly heavy)
  - Photography: full-bleed (editorial photography hero bands with massive uppercase display)
  - Motion: eased
  - Color temp: high-contrast (scarlet red CTA on white/photography)
  - Shadow: single-tier
  - Border: invisible
- **Vertical fit hipotético:** mass-market business, agency-funnel pro, corporate-consumer hybrid
- **Por que selecionada:** representa "heavy display sans + signature red on photography" — combo classic telecom/agency. Wise tem weight 900 mas magazine fintech; Vodafone é mass-market photography-driven. Nike é athletic uppercase mas Futura editorial. Vodafone preenche "corporate-consumer mass-market heavy" único.
- **Marcas descartadas com vibe similar:** mastercard (warm cream + orbit + black CTA — also distinct), bmw (cream cream automotive corporate), uber (b&w geometric mas mais sober)
